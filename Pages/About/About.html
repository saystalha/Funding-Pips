Our Mission

Founded by experienced professional traders, FundingPips was established on the principle of partnership. We recognized the barriers faced by retail traders—limited capital, emotional stress, and lack of structure—and built a firm designed to systematically overcome them. Our mission is to identify, fund, and mentor the next generation of trading talent, creating a mutually beneficial ecosystem where success is shared.

Transparency

We operate with clear, straightforward rules and no hidden fees. Trust is the foundation of our community.

Innovation

Continuously improving our platform and offering the best trading conditions for our global pool of talent.

Community

A thriving global community supported by dedicated resources and mentorship from professional traders.

Every feature, from our cutting-edge risk management tools to our comprehensive educational resources, is designed to empower you. We succeed when you succeed, and that shared vision drives everything we do.
